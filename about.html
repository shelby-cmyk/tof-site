We Built TOF to Fix
a Broken Industry.

Identity data was fragmented, stale, and inaccessible. We changed that.

Our Mission

Make Consumer Identity
Accessible and Trustworthy

"We believe that every organization deserves access to accurate, fresh, and actionable identity data — without the opacity, lock-in, and inaccuracy of legacy providers."

TOF was founded on a simple conviction: the data industry had a quality problem. Match rates were inflated. Data was months out of date. Activation required months-long implementations. And pricing was anything but transparent.

We built the platform we wish had existed when we were on the other side of the table — as practitioners trying to understand our customers and reach them with precision.

The Founding Story

TOF was started in 2021 by a team of data infrastructure veterans who had spent a combined 30+ years building and buying identity data at companies like Acxiom, Epsilon, LiveRamp, and Google.

We watched enterprises pay millions for consumer data that was 60 days stale and matched at under 70%. We saw marketing teams activate through integrations that took 90 days to stand up. We saw compliance teams scrambling to meet CCPA obligations with systems that weren't designed for them.

So we left to build something better. TOF launched its first consumer graph in Q3 2022 and has grown to serve 200+ enterprise customers across financial services, retail, healthcare, and media.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Four principles that shape every product decision, every customer interaction, and every hire we make.

01

Precision Over Volume

We'd rather have 300M highly accurate records than 500M questionable ones. Quality is the only metric that matters to us.

02

Privacy by Design

Compliance isn't a checkbox — it's architecture. Every data product we build starts with consent, suppression, and right-to-delete built in.

03

Speed to Activation

If a customer can't use our data in days, we've failed. From API keys to activated audiences, we eliminate every unnecessary step.

04

Customer Obsession

Our success is entirely downstream of yours. We measure our team not on contracts signed, but on outcomes delivered to our customers.
