How it works

Three steps. No new stack.

Drop one tag. We do the resolution, the hygiene, and the delivery into the tools you already run.

01 / CAPTURE

The pixel fires

One JavaScript tag via GTM or hardcoded. On page load it captures hashed device, cookie and network signals — plus any first-party fields already on the page. No new infrastructure.

02 / RESOLVE

Matched to the Spine

Signals resolve against the TOF Identity Spine — 16 deterministic layers across 98% of U.S. adults. Output as hashed match, full PII, or PII + phone + intent, depending on your tier.

03 / DELIVER

Clean records, your stack

Every record is scrubbed against suppression before it leaves us, then delivered real-time by webhook, to S3/SFTP, or into Klaviyo, HubSpot, GHL and other destinations.

Why Spine Pixel

We own the graph the others rent

It's our identity graph

Most visitor-ID tools resolve against a leased third-party graph. Spine Pixel runs on the TOF Identity Spine — the same deterministic asset behind our data business. Higher match rates, no middleman markup.

Suppression is built in

Resolved records are scrubbed against suppression before delivery — opt-outs, DNC, your own exclusion lists. Nobody else bundles hygiene with resolution. It's a match-quality win and a liability shield.

Intent comes along

Layer Keyword Custom Audiences intent onto every resolved visitor. You don't just learn who they are — you learn what they're in-market for, so you can prioritize and personalize.

One graph, every channel

The pixel is the front door. The same resolved identity flows into retargeting, direct mail, reactivation and lookalikes — one consistent identity across the whole funnel.
